Instruction

Everyone knows one piece of information unique to them, and everyone wants to know everything.

The goal of the game is to do this in the fewest moves possible. The amount of people you start with can be changed using the slider.

To actually play, you can drag and drop one person to another, which simulates a conversation between the two of them.

During this conversation,the two people involved will talk, exchanging all the information they already know, including information they acquired in other conversations.

After a conversation between two people, a line will form between them, indicating that they've already conversed, meaning that they can't converse again.

The number next to someone's picture indicates how much information someone knows, and hovering over it displays exactly what gossip the person knows, labeled by the gossip originator.
